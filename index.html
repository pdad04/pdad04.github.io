About Me

I have always enjoyed a challenge and solving problems, and although my work has given me the chance to do that, I wanted to step into something that offered bigger challenges and a chance to make a bigger impact on the work that I do. That is why I decided to make the move into software development.

I have spent years working directly with engineering, product managers and customers to help bring new products and services to market. I am highly motivated and driven to continue to solve challenging problems that make a positive impact while continuing to improve on my skill set as a developer.

Currently, my focus is mainly on Frontend work with React being my framework of choice. Though my focus is frontend I have also done some backend work with Rails and recently began learning NodeJS. Below you will find the current technologies that I have worked with.

Rental Watch
Project Code
Rental Watch

I built this as a React Native iOS app. The purpose of this app is simply a way to keep track of movies that I want to watch when they become available for rent; keeping track with pen and paper or in my notes app wasn't cutting it. The app allows searching the iTunes store and adding movies to your watch list so you can see when the movie becomes available.

I used the following for this project

  • React Native
  • Redux
  • IgniteCLI
